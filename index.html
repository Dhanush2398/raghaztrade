Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about Raghaz Trade, MLM & earnings

Raghaz Trade works on a multi-level marketing model. When you sell a product or refer someone, you earn commissions. You also get rewards from the sales made by your downline (your referrals, their referrals, and so on).

For example, if a product costs ₹123, the company keeps ₹45. The remaining ₹75 is distributed as commission: Parent earns ₹39, Grandparent earns ₹23, and Great-Grandparent earns ₹16.4.

Simply click on the Register button, create your account, and purchase your first product. Once registered, you can start referring people and earning commissions instantly.

You can withdraw your earnings directly to your bank account or UPI. Once your balance reaches the minimum withdrawal amount, you can request a payout from your dashboard.
